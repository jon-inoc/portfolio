Who am I?

Hello! I'm John, a passionate and experienced game and software developer with history of working in the game and IT service industry. Has deployed mobile games and apps in Apple Store and Play Store. Proficiency in Game and Mobile Development. Knowledgable in iOS (Swift and Obj-C/C++), Android (Java, Kotlin), C++ and C#. Strong software development discipline, detail-oriented and team player. Obtained my Advance Diploma in Game Programming at Centennial College, Canada and Bachelor of Science (BS) in Computer Engineering at University of San Carlos, Philippines.

Aside from my professional and academic career, I also develop games and apps in my free time, exploring new ideas and concepts. I have a keen interest in game design, AI programming, multiplayer game mechanics and creating enjoyable and challenging level environments.

I enjoy playing basketball and badminton, and I'm also hobbyist gamer, especially with RPGs, shooters, and retro titles. I also enjoy watching anime and movies during my free time. A nature lover that enjoys camping and exploring the outdoors.
